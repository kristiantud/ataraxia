INACTION IS AN ACTION

a reminder to myself that i am not where i want to be in life because of nobody but myself -- using tautology

00:44 A.M. - 1/17/2022

--

things to note

- i want to reach my highest self
- to reach my best self, i have to put in the work
- putting in the work is hard

--

claim

inaction is an action

--

information

i am reminded that i still have to get my workout in for today (or do homework, walk the dog. this can be whatever, really)

acting on this information
 if i do my workout even though i don't want to, then i am a step closer towards being more disciplined.
if i am disciplined, then i am able to choose delayed gratification.
if i choose delayed gratification, then i will one day reap the benefits and reach my highest self.

not acting on this information
 if i don't do my workout even though i don't want to, then i am a step further from being more disciplined.
if i am not disciplined, then i become a slave to earthly desires and immediate pleasures.
if i am unable to resist immediate pleasures, then i will never reach my best self.

--

conclusion

if action implies becoming my best self,
and inaction implies not becoming my best self,
then inaction is an action
